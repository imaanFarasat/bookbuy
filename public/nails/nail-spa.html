What is a gel manicure?

A gel manicure uses a special type of polish that is cured under a UV or LED lamp, resulting in a long-lasting, chip-resistant finish.

How long does a gel manicure last?

Gel manicures typically last for two to three weeks without chipping or peeling.

What is acrylic nails?

Acrylic nails are created by combining a liquid monomer and a powder polymer, which hardens to form a durable artificial nail extension.

How often should I get a pedicure?

Getting a pedicure every four to six weeks is generally recommended to maintain foot health and hygiene.

What is dip powder?

Dip powder is a type of nail enhancement that involves dipping the nails into a coloured powder, creating a durable and long-lasting finish.

How much should I tip at a nail spa?

Tipping 15-20% of the service price is customary at nail spas.

What should I do if I have a nail infection?

If you have a nail infection, it's important to consult a dermatologist or podiatrist before getting any nail services.

Can I bring my own nail polish to a nail spa?

Most nail spas allow you to bring your own nail polish, but it's always best to confirm beforehand.

What is the difference between a regular manicure and a spa manicure?

A spa manicure typically includes extra pampering treatments like exfoliation, massage, and paraffin wax.

How can I make my manicure last longer?

Avoid prolonged exposure to water, wear gloves when doing chores, and apply cuticle oil regularly to help your manicure last longer.

What is ombre nail art?

Ombre nail art involves blending two or more colours together to create a gradient effect on the nails.

What is chrome nail polish?

Chrome nail polish creates a mirror-like, reflective finish on the nails.

What are nail wraps?

Nail wraps are adhesive stickers or films that are applied to the nails for decoration or to extend the life of a manicure.

What is the best way to remove gel polish?

Soaking the nails in acetone and gently scraping off the gel polish is the recommended removal method. Avoid peeling or picking, which can damage the natural nails.

How can I choose the right nail shape for my hands?

Consider your finger length and personal preference when choosing a nail shape. Oval, almond, and square shapes are popular choices.

Are there any health risks associated with acrylic nails?

While generally safe, acrylic nails can sometimes cause allergic reactions or damage the natural nails if not applied or removed properly. Choose a reputable salon with experienced technicians.

What is the difference between a French manicure and an American manicure?

A French manicure features white tips on a natural pink or beige base, while an American manicure uses a more natural off-white tip color.

What should I look for in a reputable nail salon?

Look for a clean and organized salon with licensed technicians, sterilized tools, and positive customer reviews.

How can I prevent my nails from breaking?

Keep your nails hydrated with cuticle oil, avoid harsh chemicals, and eat a balanced diet to promote strong and healthy nails.

What is the best way to find a nail spa near me?

Use online search engines, review websites, and social media to find nail spas in your area. Read reviews and compare services to find the best fit for your needs.
