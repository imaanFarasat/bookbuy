Easy Christmas nail designs for beginners include snowflakes, candy canes, polka dots, and simple glitter accents.

How can I remove Christmas nail polish with glitter?

Soak cotton balls in acetone, place them on your nails, and wrap them in foil for a few minutes to effectively remove glitter nail polish.
