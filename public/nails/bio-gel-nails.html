what is bio gel nails?

Bio gel nails are a type of nail enhancement that uses a flexible, soak-off gel. They offer a natural look and feel, and are considered a healthier alternative to traditional acrylics. Bio gel is gentler on your natural nails, causing less damage during application and removal.

The gel is cured under a UV or LED lamp, creating a durable and long-lasting manicure. Bio gel can be used for overlays on natural nails, tip extensions, and sculpted nail enhancements.

bio gel sculpture nails

Bio gel sculpture nails are created by building the nail extension using forms and the bio gel itself. This method allows for customized length and shape, perfect for achieving dramatic or unique nail designs.

how to remove bio gel nails at home

While professional removal is recommended, you can remove bio gel nails at home. File the top layer of the gel to break the seal. Soak cotton balls in acetone and place them on each nail, wrapping with foil.

After 10-15 minutes, gently push off the softened gel with a cuticle pusher. Avoid peeling, as this can damage your natural nails.

bio gel nails vs acrylic

Bio gel is a more flexible and natural-looking alternative to acrylic. It's also gentler on the natural nail and doesn't have the strong odor associated with acrylics. While both offer durability, bio gel is typically thinner and more lightweight.

Acrylics can be more damaging to the natural nail due to the harsh chemicals and filing involved.

how long do bio gel nails last

With proper care, bio gel nails can last up to three weeks without chipping or lifting. Regular fills are recommended every 2-3 weeks to maintain their appearance and prevent breakage.

Proper nail care practices like moisturizing and avoiding harsh chemicals will help extend the life of your bio gel manicure.

What are the benefits of bio gel nails?

Bio gel nails are more flexible, natural-looking, and gentler on your natural nails compared to acrylics. They are also odorless and easier to remove.

How long do bio gel nails take to apply?

The application process typically takes around 1-2 hours depending on the complexity of the design.

Can I apply bio gel nails at home?

While it's possible, it's recommended to go to a professional salon for the best results.

How much do bio gel nails cost?

The price varies depending on the salon and design, but it typically ranges from $40-$80.

How often should I get bio gel fills?

Fills are recommended every 2-3 weeks to maintain the appearance and prevent breakage.

Can I use regular nail polish on bio gel nails?

Yes, you can use regular nail polish and remove it with non-acetone remover.

Are bio gel nails damaging to natural nails?

Bio gel is less damaging than acrylics, but improper removal can still cause some damage.

What is the difference between bio gel and gel polish?

Bio gel is thicker and stronger than gel polish, allowing for nail extensions and sculpting.

Can you swim with bio gel nails?

Yes, bio gel nails are water-resistant, so swimming is not a problem.

What is the best way to remove bio gel nails?

Soaking the nails in acetone and wrapping them in foil is the most effective way to remove bio gel.

How do I care for my bio gel nails?

Keep your nails moisturized, avoid harsh chemicals, and wear gloves when doing housework.

Can I paint over my bio gel nails?

Yes, you can apply regular nail polish over bio gel nails.

Do bio gel nails need UV light?

Yes, bio gel needs to be cured under a UV or LED lamp.

Is bio gel the same as hard gel?

Bio gel and hard gel are similar, but bio gel is typically more flexible and easier to remove.

Are bio gel nails vegan?

Most bio gel products are vegan and cruelty-free, but it's always best to check the specific brand.

What should I do if my bio gel nail breaks?

Visit a salon to have the broken nail repaired professionally.

Can I file my bio gel nails?

You can file your bio gel nails, but be gentle to avoid thinning them too much.

Can I use a cuticle pusher on bio gel nails?

Yes, you can use a cuticle pusher to gently push back cuticles.

Can I get different designs with bio gel nails?

Yes, bio gel nails can be customized with various colours, designs, and embellishments.

How can I make my bio gel nails last longer?

Proper nail care and regular fills are essential for making your bio gel manicure last longer.
