Prepping Your Nails for a Flawless Red Manicure

Proper nail preparation is key to a flawless red manicure. Begin by gently filing and shaping your nails. Push back your cuticles and remove any excess skin for a clean, polished look.

  • Soak your nails in warm water to soften cuticles.
  • Use a cuticle pusher to gently push back cuticles.
  • Trim any hangnails with cuticle nippers.

Applying Red Nail Polish Like a Pro

Applying red nail polish requires a steady hand and precision. Start by applying a thin base coat to protect your nails and prevent staining. Then, apply two thin coats of red polish, allowing each coat to dry completely before applying the next. Finish with a top coat for a long-lasting, chip-resistant manicure.

  • Use three strokes to apply each coat of polish.
  • Clean up any smudges with a cotton swab dipped in nail polish remover.
  • Seal the edges of your nails with top coat for extra protection.

What is the best way to remove red nail polish without staining my nails?

Use a non-acetone remover and hold a cotton pad soaked in remover on each nail for a few seconds before wiping.
